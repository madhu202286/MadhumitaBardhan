Data Professionals Survey

OVERVIEW

In this project, I analysed the responses of a survey conducted for data professionals. My main goal was to learn navigating thorugh Power BI and creating a dynamic dashboard. This dashboard shows various important insights from the survey-takers.


RESOURCES & TOOLS

1. Power BI

DATA CLEANING

The data cleaning part, I completed within excel. I refined the columns: Country, Favourite Programming Language and Average Salary

DATA ANALYSIS

Next I created a box plot, a stacked bar chart, 2 speedometer charts, a donut chart and a horizontal bar chart to gather some insights from the survey such as average salary, average age, difficulty in breaking into the field of data, favourite programming language, happiness with job etc. I also classified the survey takers by region.

DATA VISUALIZATION

I organised all the sheets to form a dashboard, and also included 2 cards displaying average age and total survey takers. I applied an appropriate theme as well. All the elements of the multicolored charts act as filters and makes the dashboard dynamic.
